What is ASA?

The Accounting Software Academy (ASA) is Kuranga Digital’s flagship EdTech initiative designed to address one of the biggest challenges in today’s labor market: the mismatch between academic education and workplace requirements.

ASA focuses on hands-on experiential learning, using modern accounting and financial management software to equip students, graduates, professionals, and entrepreneurs with job-ready digital finance skills. The program emphasizes real business scenarios, practical exercises, and industry-aligned tools to ensure participants gain confidence, competence, and employability.

ASA is not just a training program — it is a career development pathway.

The Problem We Solve

Technology is transforming accounting, finance, and business operations faster than traditional academic curricula can adapt. As a result:

Graduates leave school without practical exposure to real accounting systems

Employers struggle to find job-ready talent

Youth unemployment increases due to skills mismatch

Businesses lack digital financial literacy and accountability

ASA directly addresses this gap by aligning education with labor market needs, ensuring learners graduate with skills that are immediately applicable in professional environments.

PROGRAM OVERVIEW

The Study Now and Pay Later Program is a structured, hands-on capacity building initiative designed to remove financial barriers while equipping participants with practical, job-ready digital, accounting, and taxation skills. The program is delivered entirely in physical, face-to-face classes with day and evening shifts for accessibility.

Over three months, participants progress through:

Month 1: Professional Excel Training (Foundation)

Beginner: Data entry, formatting, basic formulas, tables

Intermediate: Functions, charts, data validation, filtering

Advanced: Pivot tables, dashboards, macros, analytics

Field Work / Practical Labs: Hands-on application using real datasets

Assessments & Certification: Weekly assessments and final exam


Month 2: Professional QuickBooks Training (Core & Advanced Modules)

Core Modules (1–6): Introduction, Company Setup, Customers & Sales, Vendors & Purchases, Banking, Inventory Management

Advanced Modules (7–12): Payroll, Financial Reporting, Taxes, End-of-Period, Advanced Features, Tips & Best Practices

Field Work / Practical Labs: Invoicing, bank reconciliation, reporting, inventory tracking

Assessments & Certification: Weekly quizzes and final practical exam
